Red Velvet Cupcakes

This red velvet cupcake recipe yields soft,
fluffy cupcakes with a rich chocliate flavor
and a vibrant red clior. Topped with a creamy and
tangy cream cheese frosting, these cupcakes
are perfect for any occasion and
are sure to impress your guests.
